To John Wark,
Founder of NSS

Thank you for giving us the opportunity to learn and grow as developers and individuals. Your knowledge and insight into the tech community in Nashville is a huge asset. We appreciate all of the work you do to check-in with students and instructors in the unique time we are in. Thank you for always prioritizing people first.

To Joe, Bryan, Madi, and Sage,
Our NSS Instructors

Thank you for all of your support, guidance, and dad jokes. You all went above and beyond to give us the full NSS experience. Whether it was through long hours of debugging, providing Zoom tour videos of the school, or dressing up to act out one of our employers. You have always selflessly gone out of your way to provide the best possible learning environment for us.

We will always be thankful to our instructors for their tireless dedication to maximizing each individual student’s learning experience.

To Ashley and Kristin,
Our Career Development Team

Thank you for all that you’ve done to adapt the career development offerings to suit the changing world we are in. You have provided excellent opportunities for us to learn about the job market we are entering and helped us form genuine connections with NSS graduates in the field. From resume feedback to mock interviews, we’ve been given the best tools we could ask for to get ourselves out there.

To all of our Guest Speakers, Roundtable Guests, and Mock Interviewers,

Thank you for taking hours out of your day to offer unique insights, career experience, and advice. Your dedication to helping NSS students walk into post-grad life is a huge comfort.

To our Cohort 40 Classmates,

When we first started our journey together, NSS had shifted classes from in-person to online. We got to know each other while simultaneously navigating the transition to Zoom classes and life in a global pandemic.

We’ve managed to create a close-knit group of people who’ve laughed, cried, and toughed out NSS together.

Thank you to everyone for bringing their own individuality, life experiences, and strengths to our Cohort!
